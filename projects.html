All My Projects

Time Failed
Time Failed is a simple mod that will tell you in the results screen what time you failed at.
Qolour Switcher
Qolour Switcher is my first mod that I developed for Beat Saber. It allows you to control the colour of your sabers, bombs and walls from the pause menu in game. Additionally, I have added timed switches that allow for the colours to change at a time set in the mod UI.
AccessAbility
AccessAbility is currently a WIP mod that gives additional settings for those who may not be able to play Beat Saber how it's intended. AccessAbility gives the user options such as disabling individual sabers and disabling crouch walls.
